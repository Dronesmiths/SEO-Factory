# Weekly SEO Task (GSC Feedback)

Goal: push pages in positions 8–20 into top 5.

1) Pull latest GSC query/page export (manual or via API)
2) Identify:
   - Queries with high impressions and position 8–20
   - Pages with high impressions and low CTR
3) For each opportunity:
   - Create 2–3 supporting pages
   - Strengthen internal links toward the target page
4) Update REGISTRY.json and sitemap.xml
5) Commit directly to main

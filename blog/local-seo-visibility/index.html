Frequently Asked Questions

Why is local SEO critical in 2026?

Because search engines have shifted to 'Hyper-Contextual' delivery, meaning service proximity is now matched with real-time semantic intent.

Does proximity still matter?

Proximity is the #1 ranking factor for local search, though topical authority can widen your reach.

What are local support pages?

They are pages targeting specific sub-cities or neighborhoods to build hyper-local relevance.

How many citations do I need?

Quality over quantity. Major data aggregators and industry-specific directories are most important.

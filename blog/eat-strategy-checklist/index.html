Frequently Asked Questions

What is E-A-T?

E-A-T stands for Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness. It's a set of guidelines used by Google search evaluators to assess the quality of content.

How often should I update my E-A-T strategy?

You should review and update your strategy at least quarterly or whenever major search engine updates occur.

Is E-A-T a ranking factor?

While not a direct ranking factor in the same way as backlinks or site speed, E-A-T is a critical component of Google's search algorithms.

Does E-A-T apply to all sites?

Yes, but it is particularly important for YMYL (Your Money Your Life) sites.

Can I automate E-A-T?

You can automate aspects of content tracking, but the demonstration of human expertise cannot be fully automated.

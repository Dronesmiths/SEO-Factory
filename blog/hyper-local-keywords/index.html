Frequently Asked Questions

What is neighborhood intent?

It's search behavior driven by local landmarks or micro-geographies rather than broad city names.

How do I find hyper-local keywords?

Use local search console data and GMB insights to see exactly what neighborhood references users are making.

Should I create a page for every neighborhood?

Only if you have unique, valuable insights or case studies for that specific area.

Do local landmarks help?

Yes, referencing landmarks helps search engines anchor your business in a physical geography.

Can I over-optimize for local?

Yes, keep the language natural and focus on actual local relevance rather than keyword stuffing.

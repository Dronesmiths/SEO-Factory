Frequently Asked Questions

What is INP?

Interaction to Next Paint (INP) measures a page's overall responsiveness to user interactions throughout the entire duration of a user's visit.

How does CLS affect ranking?

High CLS causes poor user experience through visual instability, which is a negative signal for Google's Page Experience ranking system.
